The Story of US

Nice to Meet You!

Lifestyle Scout online magazine is about living life authentic. It aims to help our readers to live happier and fuller with articles that will inspire them to have a healthy lifestyle. Due to the pandemic, I notice a lot of people are focusing on their mental health during the quarantine by changing their unpleasant habits into the good ones, improving their selves and specifically starting to do the decluttering around their houses. As my experience during the quarantine, I watched a lot of series on Netflix about self-help documentaries as well as self-help books. The authors that inspired me to make a lifestyle magazine are Marie Kondo who is famous for her organizing method, Joshua Fields Milburn and Ryan Nicodemus known as The Minimalist that promotes a minimalist lifestyle, Nadiya Hussain who also has a Netflix Show for her cooking skills and sharing her recipes perfect for busy lives and lastly, I also got inspired by Haemin Sunim a famous monk and author of Love for Imperfect Things, after reading his book I was enlightened by his philosophies in life. So, with these guides I was able to improve myself more and I would like to take this opportunity to share their works. .
