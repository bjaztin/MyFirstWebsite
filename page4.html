Slow Down and Embrace Our Imperfection According to Haemin Sunim

By Jaztin Buenaventura | February 27, 2020

Haemin Sunim lived in New York city, He was a celebrity buddhist monk, he was so popular because of his book, Love for Imperfect Things & The Things You Can See Only When You Slow Down.

The point of one of his books, The Love for Imprefect Things is to accept our flaws and remember the importance of loving ourselves in a imperfect world. The book explained how the expectations fall short. We often forgot how to appreciate our life and live in the moment instead of aiming for something greater everytime we achieved sucess. There is no shame in striving for more but forgeting to enjoy the moment and not being contented must be change.

The book reminds us that it is okay if we have not achieved our dreams yet, it is okay to not be okay and what really matters is that we are able to feel joy, accept and share our love to others by loving ourselves first.

Learnig to love ourselves with imperfections is very diffucult to most of people. They tend to project their insecurities sometime to others and use this as their defence mechanism. AS the en master Sengchan quote "True freedom is being without anxiety about imperfection" If we can never accept our flaws then we will never kmow the true freedom. We should have know ourselves better than anyone, improve ourselves with wisdom through experience. In this ways, we can embrace our own imperfection and use this as a motivation and learn something new to gain our confidence.

"Sometimes I need to read my own books for my own advice"

Sunim also had a tough life but as he experienced this challenges, he was able to cope up and realize that there is more to life if we are enough brave to search for it. Nowadays, a lot of people are in thir comfort zone because they are afraid of problems they are might face in but Sunim said that the point of living is to learn from our mistakes and use that as a bleesings as we have the opportunity to learn from it. Life is not always pretty bu there is always light on every darknest.

After reading this article, I am sure you will read his books> For my experience, it is my most favorite book I have read in my entire life. If you feel lost, I suggested to read his books to enlighted you up.
